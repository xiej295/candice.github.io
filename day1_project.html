D-WORD CLOUD

Basic Introduction

It is a word cloud made for D12. Our daily study is inseparable from the canvas. We use canvas to upload assignments, discuss, view slides, send and receive notifications and emails. Therefore, the canvas is full of the text related to recent courses and projects. For example, the most recent keywords are 7 in 7, arduino, 1D to 4D, and also The Three-Body Problem. So, I use processing to extract the text on the canvas, and then count the number of the occurrences of each word, and finally display it in the form of word cloud. It can be used as the background of the canvas's home page. When you feel the same about a word, you can click on it. It will become larger as the number increases.







D-POWER STRIP

Basic Introduction

It is a power strip designed for D12. Because there aren't enough power souce to charge your computer and other electronic devices. Most of the power sources are far away from our seats. Also, most of the people use Macbook and the charger of the Macbook could take up a really large space. So, I came up with such an idea that the power strips are connected to the nearyby power sources, people can move the power strip along the pole according to their seats. There are two charging ports on each power strip. One is for your computer, the other is for your phone. That could be enough.

D-Lamp

Basic Introduction

It is a lamp designed for D12. It hangs on the wall and is connected to a hook below. When nothing is hanging on the hook, the lamp is facing upwards. When something hangs on the hook, the light automatically turns on and turns to downwards, illuminating the table below. The idea of this design comes from that there is no hook to hold our bags in D12, so students can only put the bag on the ground or on the table. When a student hangs the bag on a nearby hook, it means that the student is sitting down to study here, so the lamp automatically lights up. Almost all the tables in D12 are close to the wall, both in the classroom or in the hall, so the lamps can achieve the effect of lighting.











D-Robot

Basic Introduction

It is a robot designed for D12. I stayed in D12 for a day to do my homework. While I am working on my project, I can hear the conversations of people who walked around. They say 'morning' to each other in the early morning. They say 'Hey, What's up?' to each other in the noon. They say 'See you tomorrow!' to each other in the night... This robot can record what people say to it and repeat again and again. If you say something specific to it, such as 'sunny', 'raining', 'homework', 'presentation', 'classes', 'weekend', it will play a corresponding piece of music. In this way, people could hear it say ‘morning’ in the morning and say goodbye to you in the evening.



D-SHELF

Basic Introduction

It is a shelf on the wall of D12. It consists of larger wooden boards and smaller wooden boards. The boards can be assembled together in different forms to form different patterns or letters. It could be used to store materials because there are a lot of materials used for prototypes in D12. Every time you pick something, you can put the board into different shapes. It could be both creative and entertaining. Also, students could communicate through these patterns and letters.
