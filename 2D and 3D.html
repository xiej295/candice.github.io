FOMO

Domain of Interest

My domain is “Fear of missing out caused by the information overload. Information overload broadly refers to various phenomena related to “too much information” and people’s limited processing abilities toward the information, which will cause the fear of missing out.
The motivation of my design works was to examine the phenomenon of the “fear of missing out caused by the information overload within the background of the ubiquitous adoption of Web-enabled mobile devices.
